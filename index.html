We, The Church

We're very familiar with the concept of “going” to church, but the Bible tells us that Christians “are” the church. In this short sermon series we will explore how Christianity does not revolve around my individual relationship with Jesus, but rather is centered on my being part of something so much bigger. There's no “I” in church! Join us as we learn how WE are the church and why that is so important.
